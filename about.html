Our purpose

Why Binaris exists

Powerful AI tools shouldn't be locked behind language barriers or expensive subscriptions. Binaris was built to level the playing field — giving everyone access to a capable, responsive AI regardless of where they live, what language they speak, or what budget they have.

We built Binaris with a specific focus on communities that are underserved by most AI products, starting with Haitian Creole speakers. The name Binaris is a nod to the binary nature of AI computation — simple at its core, powerful in practice.
